Privacy Policy

Computer Teacher — Complete Exam Preparation App

Effective Date: 19 June 2026 Last Updated: 19 June 2026

Computer Teacher ("the App", "we", "us", "our") is a quiz and exam-preparation application designed to help students practice computer science and general subjects. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and the choices available to you. We built this App with a simple principle: your study data stays on your device.

In short: Computer Teacher does not have a server, does not require an account, and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All your progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your phone.

1Information We Do Not Collect

Computer Teacher does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts, calendar, or other device data
  • Advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking data
  • Payment or financial information (the App does not process payments)

The App does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services that transmit data off your device.

2Information Stored on Your Device

To provide quiz history, streaks, and personalization, the App stores a small amount of data locally using Android/iOS local storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

DataPurpose
Display nameShown on the Home and Settings screens (e.g. "Hello, Rahul")
Quiz attempts & answersCalculates your score, accuracy, and lets you review past attempts
Bookmarked subjectsRemembers subjects you saved for quick access
Subject visit countsUsed only for in-app statistics
Study streak & last active datePowers the daily streak counter
Theme preference (Light/Dark)Remembers your display preference

You can permanently erase all of this data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option inside Settings, or by uninstalling the App.

3Quiz Content

Question banks (subjects, questions, options, and explanations) are bundled with the App as local files and loaded directly from the App package. No content is downloaded from or sent to an external server while you take a quiz.

4Children's Privacy

Computer Teacher is intended for students preparing for competitive and academic exams and may be used by children under the guidance of a parent, guardian, or teacher. Because the App collects no personal information from any user — adult or child — there is no special data-collection risk associated with use by minors. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, you may contact us using the details below.

5Permissions

The App may request only the minimum device permissions required for it to function correctly (such as storage access for caching quiz data). It does not request permissions for camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

6Data Sharing & Third Parties

Because no data is collected or transmitted, we have nothing to share, sell, rent, or disclose to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party. The App does not integrate third-party advertising or analytics libraries.

7Data Security

All App data is stored within your device's app-sandboxed local storage, protected by your device's operating system and your own device security (PIN, biometrics, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's OS updated for the best protection.

8Your Choices & Rights

  • View your data: All your stored data is visible within the App's Progress and Settings screens.
  • Delete your data: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings, or uninstall the App, to permanently remove all locally stored data.
  • No account required: Since there is no login system, there is no account to delete on any server.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App's features. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
